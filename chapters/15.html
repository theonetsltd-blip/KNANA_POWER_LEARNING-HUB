CHAPTER 15

Presentation Skills

Create engaging presentations using PowerPoint or LibreOffice Impress

Duration
6 hours
Level
NQF 3
Credits
2 units

Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Create presentations and slides
  • ✓ Add text, images, and multimedia
  • ✓ Use design templates and themes
  • ✓ Add animations and transitions
  • ✓ Present effectively

15.1 Creating Presentations

Presentations deliver information in visual format.

Steps to Start:

  1. Open PowerPoint
  2. Select blank presentation
  3. Choose slide layout
  4. Add title and content

15.2 Slide Design & Content

Design Best Practices:

  • ✓ Use consistent fonts and colors
  • ✓ Keep slides uncluttered
  • ✓ Use high-quality images
  • ✓ Avoid too much text (5-6 lines max)
  • ✓ Use readable font sizes (24pt minimum)
Slide TypeUse For
Title SlideIntroduction and topic
Title & ContentMain information
Two ContentCompare/contrast ideas
Picture SlideImage-focused content

15.3 Adding Animations & Transitions

Animations make presentations more engaging.

Common Transitions:

  • Fade - Smooth transition between slides
  • Wipe - One slide pushes out the other
  • Push - Slide moves in from edge

Presentation Tips

  • ✓ Speak clearly and maintain eye contact
  • ✓ Avoid reading slides word-for-word
  • ✓ Practice before presenting
  • ✓ Use presenter view to see notes
