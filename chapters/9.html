9.2 Creating Strong Passwords

A strong password has:

  • At least 8 characters
  • Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Numbers (0-9)
  • Special characters (!@#$%)

✓ GOOD: MyPass2024!

✗ WEAK: password123

9.3 Recognizing Phishing Scams

Phishing is when attackers impersonate trusted organizations to steal information.

Warning Signs:

  • ⚠️ Unexpected emails asking for personal info
  • ⚠️ Suspicious sender address
  • ⚠️ Links that don't match displayed text
  • ⚠️ Urgent requests for passwords or payment
  • ⚠️ Poor spelling and grammar

Safe Computing Practices

  1. Keep Windows and software updated
  2. Use antivirus software (Windows Defender)
  3. Enable Windows Firewall
  4. Never open suspicious email attachments
  5. Use strong, unique passwords
  6. Regularly backup your important files
