CHAPTER 17

Social Media & Digital Communication

Communicate effectively and responsibly on social platforms

Duration
5 hours
Level
NQF 2
Credits
2 units

Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Understand social media platforms
  • ✓ Create and manage social media accounts
  • ✓ Use messaging applications responsibly
  • ✓ Protect personal information online
  • ✓ Practice digital citizenship

17.1 Popular Social Media Platforms

PlatformPurposeFeatures
FacebookSocial networkingPosts, photos, groups
WhatsAppMessagingChat, calls, groups
TwitterNews & updatesTweets, hashtags, retweets
InstagramPhoto sharingPhotos, stories, reels

17.2 Creating Social Media Accounts

Basic Steps:

  1. Go to the platform website
  2. Click 'Sign Up' or 'Create Account'
  3. Enter email address and create password
  4. Complete profile with name and photo
  5. Verify email (click link in email)

17.3 Digital Citizenship & Online Behavior

Good Online Practices:

  • ✓ Treat others respectfully online
  • ✓ Don't share personal information (address, phone)
  • ✓ Avoid cyberbullying and harassment
  • ✓ Verify information before sharing
  • ✓ Use privacy settings to limit who sees content

⚠️ WARNING: Once posted online, content is permanent. Think before sharing!

Privacy Settings & Account Security

  • ✓ Set profile to private
  • ✓ Limit who can contact you
  • ✓ Enable two-factor authentication
  • ✓ Review friends/followers list regularly
