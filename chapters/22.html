22.1 Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI enables computers to learn from data and make decisions.

AI Applications:

  • Chatbots - Customer service automation
  • Recommendation Systems - Netflix, YouTube suggestions
  • Virtual Assistants - Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant
  • Image Recognition - Medical diagnosis, security

22.2 Cloud Computing

Cloud computing provides on-demand access to computing resources.

Cloud Service Models:

ModelProvider ControlsExample
SaaSApplicationsGoogle Workspace, Office 365
PaaSPlatform & ToolsHeroku, AWS Elastic
IaaSInfrastructureAWS, Microsoft Azure

22.3 Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT connects everyday devices to the internet.

IoT Examples:

  • Smart Homes - Connected lights, thermostats, doorbells
  • Wearables - Fitness trackers, smartwatches
  • Industrial IoT - Monitoring equipment, predictive maintenance
  • Smart Cities - Traffic management, energy optimization
