Email Fundamentals

Send and receive messages professionally using email

Duration
5 hours
Level
NQF 2

2 units

Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Create and configure email accounts
  • ✓ Send and receive email messages
  • ✓ Attach files to emails
  • ✓ Organize emails in folders
  • ✓ Write professional emails

11.1 Setting Up Email

You need an email account to send and receive messages.

Popular Email Providers:

  • Gmail (google.com) - 15GB free storage
  • Outlook (outlook.com) - 50GB free storage
  • Yahoo Mail (yahoo.com) - 1TB free storage

11.2 Sending and Receiving Emails

Steps to Send an Email:

  1. Click 'Compose' or 'New Message'
  2. Enter recipient's email address in 'To' field
  3. Enter subject line
  4. Type message body
  5. Click 'Send'

Email Components:

FieldPurpose
ToPrimary recipient email address
CC (Carbon Copy)Send copy to other recipients
BCC (Blind Carbon Copy)Hidden recipient copies
SubjectBrief topic of email

11.3 Attachments and Organization

Attachments allow you to send files via email.

Attaching Files:

  • Click 'Attach' or paperclip icon
  • Select file from your computer
  • Click 'Open' to attach
  • Send email as normal

⚠️ NOTE: Most email providers limit attachment size (usually 25MB)

Professional Email Etiquette

  • ✓ Use clear, professional subject lines
  • ✓ Begin with proper greeting
  • ✓ Keep messages concise and organized
  • ✓ Use proper closing
  • ✓ Proofread before sending
