CHAPTER 21

Project Evaluation & Reflection

Evaluate project success and learn from experiences

Duration
5 hours
Level
NQF 3
Credits
2 units

Learning Outcomes

  • ✓ Evaluate project success against objectives
  • ✓ Conduct self-assessment
  • ✓ Gather peer feedback
  • ✓ Document lessons learned
  • ✓ Create improvement plans

21.1 Project Success Criteria

Determine if the project met its goals.

Key Metrics:

  • Scope - Were all requirements completed?
  • Time - Was it finished on schedule?
  • Budget - Did it stay within budget?
  • Quality - Does the output meet standards?

21.2 Self-Assessment & Peer Review

Self-Assessment Questions:

  1. What skills did I develop?
  2. What challenges did I face?
  3. How did I contribute to the team?
  4. What would I do differently?
  5. What will I apply in future projects?

21.3 Lessons Learned Documentation

Document what worked well and what didn't.

Lessons Report Format:

SectionDescription
What Went WellSuccesses and achievements
What Didn't WorkChallenges and failures
Why?Root causes
Action ItemsHow to improve next time

Continuous Improvement

  • ✓ Review successes and celebrate achievements
  • ✓ Analyze failures for learning opportunities
  • ✓ Share knowledge with team and organization
  • ✓ Update processes based on findings
  • ✓ Apply lessons to future projects
